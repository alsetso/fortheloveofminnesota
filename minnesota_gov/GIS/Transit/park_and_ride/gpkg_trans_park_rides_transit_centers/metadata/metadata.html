Section 1: Overview

Originator:Metro Transit

Title: Park & Rides and Transit Centers

Abstract: The Park & Rides and Transit Centers layer contains a comprehensive inventory of park and ride and transit center facilities throughout the Twin Cities Metropolitan area and surrounding counties. Park & Rides are defined as facilities with transit service and parking available, for transit customer and carpool use. Transit Centers are defined as facilities where: two or more routes connect to transfer passengers, buses connect off street (or in clearly designated on-street spaces), there are marked 'gates' for different routes and directions, the location serves a major activity center, and there is a standing facility, or in some cases, a collection of bus shelters.

The dataset was geocoded using TLG centerline streets. When specific site details were available, the geography was moved off street lines to actual facility locations using orthophotographic imagery as a base layer. All attempts were made to correctly reflect the real lot locations; however the positional accuracy of this data cannot be guaranteed.

The Park-and-Ride Annual Survey lookup table contains information about the capacity and usage at each park-and-ride facility. This table does not include data regarding transit center facilities. Capacity and usage at Park and Pool lots, which are found in a seperate geographic dataset, are also recorded in the Park-and-Ride Annual Survey Table.

Attribute information is considered current to the metadata date and will be updated as needed.

Purpose: To identify all Park-and-Ride and Transit Center locations in the Twin Cities metropolitan area and provide historic parking utilization data for park-and-ride facilities.

Section 5: Attributes

Overview: Park & Rides and Transit Centers Layer Attributes

TCode: Unique value used by Metro Transit for identification of facilities

PnRNum: Annual parking count unique facility identification number

Name: Name of park and ride or transit center facility

FcltyType: Classification of transit facility:
PR = Park and Ride facility
TC = Transit Center facility
TC_w_PR = Transit Center with Park-and-Ride facility

Status: Current status of the facility
Open = Facility is open for use
Closed = Facility is permantly closed
Inactive = Facility is not permanently closed, but is not actively being used.
Future = Plannned future facility (determined by if a facility has been funded, or associated with a transitway project that has a locally-preferred alternative)
Open - Planned Closure = Facility is currently open, and is expect to formally close with an upcoming service change.
Inactive - Planned Closure = Facility is not currently in use, and is expected to formally close.
Inactive - Planned Reopening = Facility is not currently in use, but is expected to reopen with a future service change.

Address: Facility address

City: City in which facility is located

Provider: Transit service provider at the facility

LocDetails: Facility location details (intersection, quadrant, portion of parking lot, etc.)

YearOpen: The year in which this facility opened for service

YearClose: The year in which this facility has closed down, if applicable

CurrentCpct: Number of Parking spaced currently available at this facility

PlanCpct: Number of parking spaces forecasted in the future, if applicable

LandOwnr: Owner of the land facility is located on

FcltyOwnr: Owner of the facility itself

SharedUse: Indicates whether the facility is shared with another use
Y = Yes, facility is shared with another non-transit use
N = No, facility is standalone and not shared with another use

ShrUseDesc: Description of the shared or complementary use of the facility, if applicable

PrkngType: Physical characteristics of the parking facility
Surface = Surface parking lot
Structured = Structured parking ramp
Surface & Structured = Combination of parking in a structured ramp and surface lot
Blank = No parking, unknown, or inactive facility

Mpls_Corr: Downtown Minneapolis express bus corridor served by the facility

Stp_Corr: Downtown St. Paul express bus corridor served by the facility

Shelter: Shelter available at this facility (either bus shelter, canopy, or structural protection from elements)
Y = Yes
N = No
Blank = Unknown or not applicable

Bench: Standalone bench present at this facility
Y = Yes
N = No
Blank = Unknown or not applicable

IndoorWt: Indoot waiting area(s) present at this facility
Y = Yes
N = No
Blank = Unknown or not applicable

TrashRec: Trash receptacle present at this facility
Y = Yes
N = No
Blank = Unknown or not applicable

DropOff: Designated drop-off area (kiss-and-ride) present at this facility
Y = Yes
N = No
Blank = Unknown or not applicable

BikeRacks: Bike parking via bike racks/loops present at this facility
Y = Yes
N = No
Blank = Unknown or not applicable

BikeLockr: Bike Lockers available for rent at this facility
Y = Yes
N = No
Blank = Unknown or not applicable

RT_Sign: Real time transit information signage present at this facility
Y = Yes
N = No
Blank = Unknown or not applicable

Notes: Any additional notes regarding this facility

ATIS_ID: the unique identifier assigned by the ATIS trip planning software used on MetroTransit.org. The field serves a key for updating park and ride data in the finder tool on the MetroTransit.org website.
